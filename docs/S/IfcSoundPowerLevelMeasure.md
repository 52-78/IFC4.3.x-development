IfcSoundPowerLevelMeasure
=========================
A sound power level measure is a measure of total radiated noise with units of
decibels with a reference value of picowatts.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  Renamed in IFC4 (was IfcSoundPowerMeasure).
