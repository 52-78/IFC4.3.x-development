IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure
============================
_IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure_ is a measure of the volume of a medium flowing
per unit time.  
  
Usually measured in m3/s.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
