IfcRelAssociatesApproval
========================
The entity _IfcRelAssociatesApproval_ is used to apply approval information
defined by _IfcApproval_, in _IfcApprovalResource_ schema, to subtypes of
_IfcRoot_.  
  
> HISTORY  New entity in IFC2x2.
