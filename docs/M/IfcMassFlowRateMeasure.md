IfcMassFlowRateMeasure
======================
_IfcMassFlowRateMeasure_ is a measure of the mass of a medium flowing per unit
time.  
  
Usually measured in kg/s.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
