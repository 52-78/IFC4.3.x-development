IfcMolecularWeightMeasure
=========================
_IfcMolecularWeightMeasure_ is a measure of molecular weight of material
(typically gas).  
  
Usually measured in g/mole.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2x.
