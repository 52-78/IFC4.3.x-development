IfcAngularVelocityMeasure
=========================
_IfcAngularVelocityMeasure_ is a measure of the velocity of a body measured in
terms of angle subtended per unit time.  
  
Usually measured in radians/s.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
