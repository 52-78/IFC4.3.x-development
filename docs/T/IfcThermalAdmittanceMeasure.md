IfcThermalAdmittanceMeasure
===========================
_IfcThermalAdmittanceMeasure_ is the measure of the ability of a surface to
smooth out temperature variations.  
  
Usually measured in Watt / m2 Kelvin.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
