IfcThermalResistanceMeasure
===========================
_IfcThermalResistanceMeasure_ is a measure of the resistance offered by a body
to the flow of energy.  
  
Usually measured in m2 Kelvin/Watt.  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
