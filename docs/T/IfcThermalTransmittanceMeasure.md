IfcThermalTransmittanceMeasure
==============================
_IfcThermalTransmittanceMeasure_ is a measure of the rate at which energy is
transmitted through a body.  
  
Usually measured in Watts/m2 Kelvin.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
