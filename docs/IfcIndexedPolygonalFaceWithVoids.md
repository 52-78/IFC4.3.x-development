IfcIndexedPolygonalFaceWithVoids
================================
The _IfcIndexedPolygonalFaceWithVoids_ is a compact representation of a planar
face with inner loops, being part of a face set.  
  
> HISTORY  New entity in IFC4 Addendum 2.
