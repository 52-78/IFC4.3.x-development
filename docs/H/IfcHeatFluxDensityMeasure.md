IfcHeatFluxDensityMeasure
=========================
IfcHeatFluxDensityMeasure is a measure of the density of heat flux within a
body.  
  
Usually measured in W/m2 (J/s m2).  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
