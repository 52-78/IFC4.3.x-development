IfcPressureMeasure
==================
_IfcPressureMeasure_ is a measure of the quantity of a medium acting on a unit
area.  
  
Usually measured in Pascals (Pa, N/m2).  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
